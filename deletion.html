Delete Your Account

Last updated: September 10, 2025

We respect your privacy and your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your IronZone account and all associated personal information, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Email us at ironzoneindia@gmail.com from your registered email address or mention your registered phone number.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include your registered email and/or phone number in the message for verification.

Once we receive your request, all your personal data (email, phone, password) will be deleted from our database within 30 days.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or concerns about account deletion or your data, please contact us at ironzoneindia@gmail.com.

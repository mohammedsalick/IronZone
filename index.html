Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

How long does it take to get my clothes back?

Our standard turnaround time is 24-48 hours. However, we also offer express service for urgent requirements at a small additional cost.

Is there a minimum order value for free pickup and delivery?

No, there is no minimum order value. We offer free pickup and delivery for all orders, regardless of size.

How do you handle delicate fabrics?

We use specialized equipment and techniques for delicate fabrics. Our experienced staff is trained to handle silk, chiffon, and other sensitive materials with extra care and appropriate temperature settings.

Do you offer any package deals for regular customers?

Yes, we offer monthly packages for regular customers with discounted rates. Please contact us to discuss the options that best suit your needs.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with our service, we'll re-press your garments at no additional cost or offer a suitable compensation.
